AWS Community Day Philippines 2020

Importance

The well-being of our members and participants is a top priority of AWS User Group Philippines. All AWS UGPh community events must be conducted in a safe and healthy environment. It is also our goal to make our first annual Conference: AWS Community Day Philippines a memorable and productive event.

Consequences

Thus, amidst the Covid-19 pandemic, the AWS User Group Philippines event organizers have made the difficult decision to cancel AWS Community Day Philippines 2020. We will instead prepare for a bigger AWS Community Day Philippines for 2021. The exact dates will be announced following the guidelines to be issued by government authorities.

Stay tuned for updates by bookmarking community.awsug.ph and AWS User Group Philippines Facebook group.
